# Valon brand asset note

Official sources reviewed: https://www.valon.ai/, https://www.valon.ai/platform, https://www.valon.ai/about, and the Valon Ashby job posting.

The official Valon wordmark and sunburst are visible on the official site, but a downloadable original asset could not be retrieved through the connected research environment. To avoid tracing or recreating the mark, the campaign uses a clearly typeset, non-official `Valon` identifier for nominative company identification. It is always paired with an independent-candidate qualifier.
