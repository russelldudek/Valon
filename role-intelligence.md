# Valon - Applied AI Strategist, New Ventures

## Source boundary

Target role: Applied AI Strategist, New Ventures at Valon Technologies.
Canonical posting: https://jobs.ashbyhq.com/Valon/7dbac7a9-78bd-4a27-b057-e39cd5124a2d

## Actual work

Own signed client engagements end to end: diagnose a regulated operation, convert an ambiguous AI mandate into a sequenced execution plan, build and ship the workflow directly, manage organizational change, own the business outcome, and return the context to Valon's platform and future engagement method.

## Company moment

Valon publicly positions ValonOS as an AI-native operating system for regulated finance, beginning with mortgage servicing. Its platform unifies system-of-record data, workflows, task management, money movement, auditability, and contextual agents. The New Ventures team is being formed while initial engagements are already signed.

## Operating tensions

1. Speed vs. auditability.
2. Client specificity vs. platform leverage.
3. Executive mandate vs. operator trust.
4. Field autonomy vs. repeatable method.
5. Immediate outcome vs. durable context return.

## Candidate thesis

Make AI transformation survive contact with the work.

Each engagement should build a working, governed workflow onsite and return a reusable platform primitive: context, workflow logic, human authority, evaluation, recovery, and change-learning.

## Signature operating model

Field-to-Platform Buildline:

1. Enter the live operation.
2. Bind outcome to authority.
3. Build the smallest real workflow.
4. Operate to proof.
5. Return the primitive to ValonOS.

## Evidence selection

- Vape-Jet: direct AI-first operating transformation across production, quality, customer success, support, engineering issue flow, ERP, CRM, telemetry, and AI-assisted workflows.
- DudeWorth: direct agentic workflow and AI-readiness design using structured context, retrieval, human judgment, escalation, evaluation, and governance rails.
- Amazon: direct launch and 24/7 operating accountability.
- Compunetics: direct technical, quality, manufacturing, and customer translation in high-reliability environments.
- ZeusVu: regulated autonomous-systems field operations with a verified 100% safety record and no FAA incidents.

## Public positioning

Lead with integrated builder-operator fit, direct execution, regulated judgment, executive/operator credibility, and a concrete work sample. Do not foreground the lack of a named production AI deployment at a forward-deployed software company; preserve that evidence boundary through accurate language and a demonstrable operating artifact.
