Field-to-Platform Buildline

A practical contract for converting a vague client AI mandate into a working workflow with explicit human authority, evidence, recovery, and platform return.

Five layers

LayerQuestionMinimum record
OutcomeWhat business result must exist, for whom, by when?Owner, baseline, target behavior
WorkWhat do operators actually do, including exceptions?Sequence, handoffs, data, incentives
AuthorityWhat may the system take, suggest, route, or never do?Permissions, checkpoints, escalation
EvidenceHow will behavior, compliance, and value be evaluated?Test set, measures, acceptance criteria
RecoveryWhat happens when model, tool, data, or organization is wrong?Fallback, rollback, incident owner

Build decision

PostureUse whenRequired evidence
Workflow firstRules and orchestration are safer than an agent.Stable sequence, deterministic controls, clear owner.
Agent assistJudgment helps but human authority stays central.Evaluation, context, review, escalation.
Bounded agentTools and decisions can be delegated within a narrow envelope.Permissions, monitoring, audit, fallback.
Do not buildValue, data, authority, or recovery is not credible.Explicit reason and next information needed.

Engagement review record

MandateOutcome, owner, baseline, target behavior
Operating truthWorkflow, exceptions, data, incentives, authority
Build choiceAgent, workflow, or rules decision and bounded architecture
ControlPermissions, checkpoints, escalation, stop conditions
ProofEvaluation set, operating measures, acceptance criteria
RecoveryFallback, rollback, incident owner, learning loop
Platform returnReusable primitive, context schema, controls, playbook
Illustrative scenario

Collections

Likely posture: agent assist with named human authority and regulated communication controls. Proof: resolution quality, exceptions, compliance, operator acceptance, and recovery. Return: reusable decision pattern and evaluation suite.

The engagement is the research lab—but the operation is the proof.Platform leverage is earned by owning the field result, not by extracting context without consequence.
